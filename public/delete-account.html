Steps to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send a deletion request from the email address associated with your סחלב account. This helps us securely verify that you are the owner of the account.

Request Deletion via Email

Data Deletion Information

Upon receiving and verifying your deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

  • Your user profile information (e.g., username, email address)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Login history and activity logs

Retention Period: Your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 14 days of your request. Anonymized data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytical purposes.
